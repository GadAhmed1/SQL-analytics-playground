## üìå Overall Insight

The analysis shows that **patient distribution is balanced by gender**, with **older adults forming the largest group**.
**Diabetes, Arthritis, and Hypertension** are the most common medical conditions.
Revenue is distributed among major **insurance providers**, and medication usage is concentrated around a few top drugs.

Optimizing patient care, resource allocation, and billing processes can significantly improve **hospital efficiency and profitability**.
