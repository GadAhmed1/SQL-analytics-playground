## üìå Overall Insight
The IMDb Top 1000 dataset reveals a strong relationship between **genre, director influence,
runtime, and commercial success**.

- High-rated movies are often linked to **experienced directors**
- **Drama and Crime** genres dominate critical acclaim
- **Action & Fantasy** genres dominate box office revenue
- Longer runtimes are frequently associated with higher-rated films
